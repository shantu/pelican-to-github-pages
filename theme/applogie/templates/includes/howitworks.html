Operating in the cloud means your organization can minimize costs, increase flexibility, and better support its customers. It also means you can easily lose control of technology spending and communication across departments. Applogie manages your cloud subscriptions to maximize value and minimize risk. Easy as 1, 2, 3..



STEP 1

Applogie discovers your cloud subscriptions via payment data. The system receives this data by connecting directly to your accounting system through its API or through an imported CSV of payment information.





STEP 2

Applogie runs your payment information against our proprietary database of cloud providers to uncover all the subscriptions across your organization. Our productized integrations pull real-time data from your cloud subscription providers, so you can start to proactively manage your organization’s cloud usage.





STEP 3

Applogie lets you sit back and relax. Our built-in analytics, alerts, reports, forecasting, and benchmarking allow your organization to effectively optimize its cloud subscription usage and expenses.
